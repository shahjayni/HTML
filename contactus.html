Need a translation or language services? Contact
Atlas Translations today.

Get in touch and let us know what you need and we can help. We are operating safely from our homes during the current COVID 19 situation. We respond quickly to all enquiries. Or use the little icons here on the website for live chat (it really is one of us) or to request a call back within 27 seconds.

We'd love to chat to you here at Atlas Translations, so please contact us. Usually, if you'd like to visit and find out more about our language services, or deliver documents, you could make an appointment so we can have somebody available to meet you and make you a cuppa. We have lovely offices in St Albans, which is 20 minutes from London by train. We also have an office near Exeter in equally lovely East Devon.

At the moment this is not really happening, but we can arrange phone calls, Zoom calls, so you can chat and see us. We can also arrange collection and delivery of important documents.

If you need to drop off donations for our Bra Bank, you can do this by bagging up the bras and labelling them for Atlas Translations, and leaving them in the postbox. This is emptied daily.

Come visit us


We love visitors! You can find us at:

Censeo House
6 St Peter's Street
St Albans AL1 3LF

(We are just next to Waterstones and opposite Boots!)

Walking or by car


We are situated in the middle of St Albans, next to Waterstones, opposite Boots, overlooking the Museum, and close to the beautiful St Albans Cathedral - pop in and see England's longest nave. There are a number of nearby NCP car parks.

By train


We are a ten minute walk from St Albans City Station (20 minute direct link from King's Cross St Pancras) and from St Albans Abbey Station (15 minutes from Watford Junction/Euston).
